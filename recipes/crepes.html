Buckwheat crepes

Description

These savory buckwheat crepes go well with any sort of condiments.

I like to put some cheese in a line across the center and fold both sides in to look like a wrap.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 ½ cups milk
  • 1 ½ cups buckwheat flour
  • 2 ½ tablespoons buckwheat flour
  • 1 ¼ cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, or more as needed, divided
